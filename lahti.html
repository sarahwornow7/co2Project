Recent CO2 Policies:

  • One of Lahti's goals is to cut CO2 emissions in half by 2025. To help achieve this goal, Lahti joined the Finnish Sustainable Communities Network (FISU). Read about all of Lahti's environmental goals here.
  • To help reduce carbon emissions, Lahti's old coal plant will be replaced with a new biofuel heating plant, reducing CO2 emissions by 500,000 metric tons per year. Learn more about this power plant here.
