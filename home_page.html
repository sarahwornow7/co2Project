ECO2Change

ECO2 Change is here to provide you with easy to access information regarding CO2 emissions in different cities all over the world.
CO2 emissions are an environmental problem which means that they are also a health problem. With the help of this website, you will be able
to understand how reducing your carbon footprint not only helps you, but can help someone thousands of miles away. Hopefully, we can come
together to make Earth a cleaner place by taking responsibility for our individual impacts.
