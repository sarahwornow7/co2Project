General Questions

Is there a difference between climate change and global warming?

Global warming refers to the overall, long-term warming of the planet over the last several decades. However
climate change encompasses global warming and also refers to the other violently changing elements in nature esp.
natural disaters (wildfires, hurricanes etc.), melting glaciers, and accelerating ice melts in the Arcitc and Antarctic.

Is the sun causing global warming?

The sun can't be causing global warming. A series of satellite reports that have been measuring the energy output
of the sun since 1978 have seen a decrease in the output of energy from the sun.

Can we still prevent climate change?

Yes! We can still prevent climate change, but it will take agreement between all countries and governments. We will
have to make policies that are radical and that the vast majority of countries can agree to. If we can't do this then
some experts fear that in three years the damage that we have done will become irreversible. Climate scientists believe that
if climate change can be brought permanently lower by the year 2020 then the runaway irreversible climate change will not be
breached.

Do scientists agree on climate change?

Yes, the vast majority of scientists agree with climate change in fact, it is not the commonly said '97%'. In actuality, the
number is much closer to 98.4% when you count authors of the papers instead of actual papers

What is the greenhouse effect?

The greenhouse effect happens when greenhouse gases gather in Earth's atmosphere and stay there for a prolonged
amount of time. The greenhouse gases are carbon dioxide, methane, nitruous oxide, water vapor (part of the precipitation cycle
so it is not harmful), and ozone. The greenhouse gases allow the sun's light to shine through onto Earth and past the
atmosphere and keeps the heat from leaving. Much in the way that a real greenhouse works, letting heat in so the plants can
photosynthesize and trapping the heat so that the plants continue to grow.



What is the most harmful greenhouse gas?

There is a lot of disagreement over what the most dangerous greenhouse gas is, but because of CO2's ability to
stay in the atmosphere for a century, its concentration, and methane's ability to turn in CO2 the cards are all in favor
of CO2 for the most destructive greenhouse gas. Make no mistake, your ability to try and decrease your footprint of any of
the gases is helpful to the environment.

Why do some areas experience cooling instead of warming?

Some areas experience cooling, because the overall effects of global warming can be contained in the phrase climate change.
Climate change refers to all of the natural disasters that happen as a result of global warming. So, some areas may experience annual
cooling because of the occurence of snowstorms, rainstorms, hailstorms etc. And even though some areas experience cooling the overall
annual temperature value for the world continues to rise.

Are humans causing global warming?

Yes. Scientists have concluded that global warming is caused by the burning of coal, oil, gas, and by agricultrue.

Is global warming actually harmful?

Yes, global warming is actually harmful, because it catalyzes extreme weather changes and natural disasters. The
disastrous effects of global warming are rising sea levels (which will cause islands and entire states to become submerged
in water by the year 2100), ocean acidification (the ocean may no longer be able to sustain some life forms by the year 2100, and
humans would be advised to not go into the water), wildfires, hurricanes, and even more sporadic natural disasters. So yes, global
warming is, in fact, harmful to us moreso than it is to the planet.



Are there benefits to global warming?

Yes, but in the grand scheme of things no. For most coutnries there is nothing to gain, but for China, they could see their dry areas beginning to get rainfall and their flooding areas begin to get drier, but these effects would come with great consequences for china and the rest of the world too. This favorable change in climate would be short-lived, because the overall negative effects of
climate change (violent disasters, melting ice caps, animal extinction) would continue to impact the globe.

Can we reverse global warming?

For now, yes, but if we wait too long the effects of global warming will become permanent. In order for us to reverse out current climate
situation we need to actually meet our carbon goals, and began to level out and lower our output of CO2 per year.

Are there any economic policies that help limit carbon emissions?

There are two options to lower emissions. The first is the carbon tax. Carbon tax levies a fee on the production and
distribution of carbon and sets a price for producing dirtier oils. The carbon tax encourages companies to use clean energy and
invest into energy efficiency. The secons option for lowering emissions is cap and trade. Cap and trade is when a cap is set
by the government and allowances are allocated to different businesses in connection with the government and the rest of the carbon is
is auctioned off to the highest bidder. Companies that use more than their allocated carbon are taxed, but if they decide not to use some
their allowed carbon then they can sell it to other companies.

What is C40?

C40 is a group of cities that connect over 600 million people to tackle climate change and reduce carbon emissions.

Diet

Do I have to be vegan?

While being vegan would decrease your carbon output exponentially, that is not the only answer to this very complex problem.
The main two dietary foods that you should cut out are red meat and dairy. Even limiting your consumption of these foods would be better.

How do I transition to a plant based diet?

It would be best if you started out slowly, so that you aren't completely overwhelmed by cravings. If you usually eat red meat everyday. Try
only eating it for four days out of the seven. When you begin to get used to this, move it down to around two days a week, and then try to stop
eating it entirely. Do the same thing with dairy if you're up for it, but make sure it's a gradual change so that you don't succumb to your cravings.

Are there any good meat replacements?

Yes! There are lots of wonderful companies that produce meat free and dairy free products, one of the best reviewed companies is
Morning Star is delicious

How do I get my protein?

There are a number of ways that people with a plant based diet get their protein. Nuts, beans, legumes, quinoa, soy products
and hummus. And, if you don't like any of these food options then there are a number of different protein powders, drinks, and bars that
are dairy free.

Home Energy

How can I make my home more energy efficient?

  • Lower your thermostat
  • Start a compost pile
  • Install reduced flow showerheads
  • Replace incandescent bulbs
  • Don't run your washer with hot water
  • Perform an energy audit
  • Install solar panels
  • Plant deciduous trees in your yard
    • These trees will provide shade in your house during the summer months
    • Will let sunshine through them in the winter months, when their branches are bare

    What lightbulbs are energy efficient?

    Change your incandescent lightbulbs to compact fluorescent bulbs or LEDs.
